Vision

To emerge as a premier institution of engineering education and research, nurturing technically competent, socially responsible and environmentally conscious professionals with human values.

Mission

  • To provide quality technical education through effective teaching-learning process.
  • To promote research and innovation in collaboration with industry.
  • To inculcate ethical values, leadership qualities and social responsibility.
  • To create state-of-the-art infrastructure for academic and research activities.

About the College

History

Established in 1992, UBDT College of Engineering has grown to become one of the premier engineering institutions in Karnataka.

Programs

Offers UG programs in CSE, ECE, EEE, ME, CE and PG programs in M.Tech, MBA and MCA.

Achievements

NAAC 'A' Grade, NBA accredited programs, Excellent placement record, Strong industry collaborations.
